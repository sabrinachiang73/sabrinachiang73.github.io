I love serving others!

A21

A21 is a nonprofit organization that fights against human trafficking.

In 2019, I participated in the Walk For Freedom where around 100 people walked across the golden gate bridge holding signs raising awareness for human trafficking.

In 2020, I hosted the Global Freedom Summit which was an online global social justice event that equipped, trained, and inspired people all around the world to fight human trafficking.

Nova Spero

Nova Spero is a not-for-profit organization that aims to empower students from local underpriveleged refugee communities across San Diego County.

My family and a family friend started this organization around two years ago. We and our team of volunteers meet up every Saturday to tutor and mentor around 25 students from underprivileged refugee communities through learning math and playing sports. Our hope is that they will feel empowered knowing that there are so many people who care for them and believe in them.

Kigali, Rwanda

During both Summer 2017 and Summer 2018, I spent two weeks in Kigali, Rwanda. I went with a team of students and teachers from my community to teach English, bring school supplies/sports equipment, and most importantly build relationships with the students at Kigali Christian Schools. We also got to spend a few days in Rwamagana, Rwanda where we helped paint a house for a family whose old house had just been destroyed in a storm.

These trips were hands down one of the best experiences of my life.
